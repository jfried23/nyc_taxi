New York City Taxi Traffic

Here we are taking a look at the famious (at least amongst data anlysists) NYC Taxi and Limousine Commission Trip Records. I will be using a subset of the total data corpus detailing 72,368,450 individual cab rides taken between January 2015 and June 2015 inclusive, and totalling ~28Gb of raw data. To bin the GPS corrdinates of each pickup and drop-off by neighborhood I used boundry shape files from the New York City Department of City Planning Neighborhood Tabulations 2010 census tracts. All the anlysis was preformed using PostgreSQL / PostGIS (for the heavy geospatial stuff) and Python / Pandas (everything else); analysis scripts can be found on my github. That out of the way and without further ado, lets take a look at the data!
